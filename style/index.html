Email Signatures

Instructions:
Right-click on image
Choose "Copy Link Address"
Go to G-Mail settings (gear icon in upper right corner of email)
Scroll down to Signature Section; click on "Insert Image" icon
Choose "Web Address URL", right click in field, choose "paste", click "select"
Return to Style page, highlight link below image, right-click, "copy"
Go back to G-Mail settings, click on image in signature; Click "Change"
Right click in "Web Address" field, select "Paste"
Click "Ok", then scroll to bottom of page, click "Save Changes"
Done!
