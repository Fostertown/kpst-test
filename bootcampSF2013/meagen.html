Meagen Eisenberg | VP of Demand Generation, DocuSign

Meagen has spent over 17 years working in the high-tech industry. In 2011 she received the Marketing Visionary Markie award within the marketing automation field. Before joining DocuSign, she was Director of WW Demand Generation at ArcSight, an HP Company, and prior to that she led worldwide programs and events at TRIRIGA (acquired by IBM) for integrated workplace management systems. Prior to TRIRIGA, she was at Postini (acquired by Google) and IBM, working in solutions and product marketing for security and compliance. Earlier she worked as an IT Engineer at Cisco Systems and Applied Materials.
