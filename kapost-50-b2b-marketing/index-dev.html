Aberdeen Group

Forward-thinking research company the Aberdeen Group is content marketing meets data science and analytics in rare form.

Born out of an integration between content marketing and research firm the Aberdeen Group, and leading technology, data science, and analytics company Market Intelligence, the new Aberdeen Group set out on a new mission to leverage their unique position in the market.

The goal was to let their prospect database know about the new Aberdeen Group and how they could utilize their services to create their own full story of content, data and analytics for success.

So naturally, they started by telling their own.

By creating a series of research reports, checklists, and white papers touting the intersection of content, data, and analytics as the key to campaign success, the Aberdeen Group moved quickly to create a wide range of engaging, informational, and at times interactive content to establish themselves as leaders in their industry.
